Lung Health – Diet & Lifestyle Guide

Foods That Help

  • Leafy Greens – Spinach, kale (antioxidants)
  • Fatty Fish – Salmon, mackerel (omega-3)
  • Turmeric & Ginger – Anti-inflammatory
  • Citrus Fruits – Vitamin C for immunity
  • Eggs & Nuts – Vitamin E protection

Foods to Avoid

  • Processed Meats – Increases inflammation
  • Sugary Drinks – Worsens lung function
  • Excessive Salt – Fluid retention
  • Fried Foods – Bloating affects breathing
  • Alcohol – Reduces lung capacity

Lifestyle Tips

  • Deep Breathing – 10 min daily exercises
  • Regular Cardio – Walking, swimming
  • Quit Smoking – #1 improvement factor
  • Stay Hydrated – 8+ glasses water/day
  • Clean Air – Use air purifiers indoors
